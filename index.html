GERMIESHIELD

Fabric Technology with 6 Functional layers for Better Protection

ANTI BACTERIAL & ANTI DROPLET OUTER LAYER

Kills 99.9% of all harmful Micro-organisms, Bacteria and Fungi. It destroys the growth of germs even up to 30 washes.

MIDDLE 4 LAYER MELT BLOWN FILTRATION PROTECTION

A 4 layered fused filter media (Spun Bond + Melt blown +Melt Blown + Spun Bond) provides >95% Particle filtration of > 0.3 micron to help protect against bacterial and low viral load.



A strong Barrier for Liquid flashes protects from dangerous droplets, dust, smog, pollen, & pathogenic micro organisms.

INNER COMFORT LAYER

A soft, completely Skin-Friendly,100% cotton fabric. The moisture wicking makes the tightly woven fabric helps in absorption of sweat giving a dry cool feel with ease in breathing in the hot weather.

ERGONOMIC DESIGN & CONTOUR FIT

The mask is ergonomically designed for snug fit and large coverage of face. This provides adequate breathing space while walking and talking.

EAR ELASTIC AND ADJUSTABLE STOPPER

Superior ear elastics on the outer mask edge avoids facial abrasion giving long term comfort. The adjustable stopper helps to fit on different face types, firmly and yet comfortably.



The outer layer is an open textured 100% polyester fabric which is breathable, molds to the face contours yet giving a comfortable fit. The better fit also creates barriers for virulent drops and improves the mask efficacy.

CONTOUR FIT

The light and flexible nose-pin fits the mask securely at the nose bridge. The sturdy wide strip avoids gripping the nose and yet giving a snug fit and prevents eye wear fogging.

WASHABLE

Gentle wash with soap and water. Handle nose-pin with care. Flat dry in clean hygienic place. Do not twist or wring mask. The finish also has stain release properties to prevent and remove tough stains.

CARE

Wash your hand properly before after wearing the mask. Do not reuse mask after removing. Wash mask after daily use. Flat fold and store in dry place away from sunlight. For individual use and not to be shared. A pack of 4 for convenient wash and use.

CERTIFIED PROTECTION AND QUALITY
